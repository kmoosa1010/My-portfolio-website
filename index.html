About Me

Moosa Kekana worked in the mining sector for 4 years + as a surveyor. During his time at Assen Iron Ore mine he collaborated closely with geologists, mine planners, and mining engineers to ensure accurate execution of plans. He accurately quantified and reported production performance for managers to make informed decisions. While working as a surveyor, he developed a strong interest in Information Communication Technology and decided to enrol for Bachelor of Science in Informatics to broaden his knowledge. He believes in self-development and continuous learning and using his diverse skills to help small businesses to grow and be successful.
